My Favorite Sports

My top 3 favorite sports are pickleball, spikeball, and volleyball.

Why These?

  1. Pickleball
    • I grew up playing tennis and have been playing pickleball for a long time. All my family plays so it's a great bonding time.
    • It's easily accessible as there are many courts to play on nearby.
    • I feel like it's one of the sports that I am best at.
  2. Spikeball
    • I love the uniqueness of a 360 degree playing field.
    • You can play this game anywhere as long as there's an open field, and someone has a net.
    • Spikeball is nostalgic as I played it the most with my family during COVID.
  3. Volleyball
    • My family used to play every weekend with other family friends.
    • I love the feeling of being able to spike the ball on somebody or to block a spike. It's very rewarding.
    • My YSA would play volleyball every week together so I got a lot of playing time with my friends.
